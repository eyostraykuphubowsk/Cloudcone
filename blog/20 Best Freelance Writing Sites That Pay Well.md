# 20 Best Freelance Writing Sites That Pay Well

Freelance writing is one of the most flexible and rewarding careers available today. However, finding the **best freelance writing site** that pays well and provides consistent opportunities can be challenging. With so many platforms to choose from, how do you determine the one that aligns with your skills and goals? In this article, we’ll explore the **20 best freelance writing sites** that not only pay well but also offer steady opportunities. Let’s dive in and find the right platform for you!

## 1. **Upwork**

Upwork is widely regarded as the **best freelance writing site** for both beginners and professionals. With thousands of writing jobs posted daily, it caters to a broad spectrum of writers. Whether you’re just starting or are highly experienced, you can bid on jobs that match your expertise.

- **Payout Structure:** Fixed-price or hourly.
- **Notable Feature:** Long-term client relationships.

## 2. **Fiverr**

Fiverr is another top contender for the **best freelance writing site**. This platform allows you to create “gigs” showcasing your writing services, and clients can hire you directly through your gig page.

- **Payout Structure:** Project-based.
- **Notable Feature:** You set your own rates.

## 3. **Freelancer**

Freelancer offers a competitive environment where you can bid on projects or participate in contests. This platform could be the **best freelance writing site** for showcasing your creativity.

- **Payout Structure:** Fixed-price, hourly, or contest-based.
- **Notable Feature:** Opportunity to win jobs through contests.

## 4. **Textbroker**

Textbroker is highly rated as a **best freelance writing site** for beginners. It connects writers with clients seeking web content and blog posts, offering pay per word. As your experience grows, your pay rate increases.

- **Payout Structure:** Per word.
- **Notable Feature:** Regular and fast payments.

## 5. **iWriter**

iWriter is a simple and beginner-friendly platform. Writers earn based on their ratings and can increase their pay with consistent, high-quality work.

- **Payout Structure:** Per article.
- **Notable Feature:** Low entry barrier for beginners.

## 6. **WriterAccess**

WriterAccess stands out as a great option for experienced writers. It connects writers with clients looking for high-quality content in various niches.

- **Payout Structure:** Per project.
- **Notable Feature:** Offers training to enhance your writing skills.

## 7. **Constant Content**

Constant Content could be the **best freelance writing site** for seasoned writers. You can submit articles to a marketplace where clients purchase them, often at premium rates.

- **Payout Structure:** Per sale.
- **Notable Feature:** You set your own prices.

## 8. **Contena**

Contena is a premium platform for those looking to scale their freelancing career. It offers access to high-paying job listings and opportunities.

- **Payout Structure:** Per project.
- **Notable Feature:** Exclusive, high-end job postings.

## 9. **ProBlogger Job Board**

ProBlogger Job Board is a trusted platform for finding high-quality blogging opportunities. Clients post jobs directly, and writers can apply to ones that match their skills.

- **Payout Structure:** Per job.
- **Notable Feature:** Respected within the blogging community.

## 10. **Scripted**

Scripted is an elite platform for top-tier freelance writers, as it accepts only the top 2% of applicants. It connects writers with high-paying clients who need professional-grade content.

- **Payout Structure:** Per project.
- **Notable Feature:** High-end client base.

## 11. **Skyword**

Skyword provides opportunities to write for large brands, making it an ideal choice for those seeking long-term contracts.

- **Payout Structure:** Per project or retainer.
- **Notable Feature:** Collaborate with Fortune 500 companies.

## 12. **Crowd Content**

Crowd Content offers consistent, quick-paying writing jobs across various niches. Writers are paid per word, and payments are processed quickly.

- **Payout Structure:** Per word.
- **Notable Feature:** Fast payments.

## 13. **Verblio**

Verblio specializes in blog writing and SEO content. Clients can purchase pre-written content or request custom projects, making it a strong option for SEO writers.

- **Payout Structure:** Per project.
- **Notable Feature:** Emphasis on SEO writing.

## 14. **Copify**

Copify offers straightforward writing assignments and is considered a reliable platform for consistent, simple writing jobs.

- **Payout Structure:** Per project.
- **Notable Feature:** Steady workflow.

## 15. **BlogMutt (now Verblio)**

BlogMutt focuses on blog content and SEO writing, making it a strong choice for freelance bloggers.

- **Payout Structure:** Per project.
- **Notable Feature:** SEO-focused content opportunities.

## 16. **ClearVoice**

ClearVoice connects writers with reputable brands and offers consistent opportunities for skilled freelancers.

- **Payout Structure:** Per project.
- **Notable Feature:** High-quality clients and consistent work.

## 17. **PeoplePerHour**

PeoplePerHour allows freelancers to bid on projects across a wide variety of writing niches. It offers flexibility in job selection.

- **Payout Structure:** Hourly or per project.
- **Notable Feature:** Flexible job selection process.

## 18. **The Writer Finder**

The Writer Finder specializes in connecting writers with startups. If you enjoy working with niche clients, this could be the **best freelance writing site** for you.

- **Payout Structure:** Per project.
- **Notable Feature:** Focused on startup clients.

## 19. **The Urban Writers**

The Urban Writers is tailored for ghostwriters, particularly those specializing in eBooks. It’s a strong platform for writers looking to explore ghostwriting opportunities.

- **Payout Structure:** Per project.
- **Notable Feature:** Specializes in eBook creation.

## 20. **Scripophily**

For finance writers, Scripophily is a niche platform that offers specialized opportunities to create finance-related content for clients.

- **Payout Structure:** Per project.
- **Notable Feature:** Focus on finance writing.

## Key Takeaways

- Platforms like **Upwork** and **Fiverr** are excellent for beginners seeking flexibility.
- Experienced writers can find high-paying opportunities on **Skyword** and **Scripted**.
- Niche platforms like **Scripophily** and **The Writer Finder** cater to specific industries.

## FAQs

**1. What is the best freelance writing site for beginners?**  
Fiverr and iWriter are great for beginners due to their low entry requirements.

**2. How much can I earn on the best freelance writing sites?**  
Experienced writers can earn anywhere from $30 to $150 per hour on platforms like Upwork and Scripted.

**3. What is the best freelance writing site for long-term contracts?**  
Skyword and Scripted are ideal for securing long-term work with reputable clients.

Freelance writing offers immense flexibility and potential. Choosing the **best freelance writing site** that aligns with your skills and goals can turn your passion into a successful career.
